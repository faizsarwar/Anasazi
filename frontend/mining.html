A streamlined process for consistent quality.

For two and a half decades, we’ve been refining our mining and production process to ensure the highest quality products, time and time again.

Our Mine

The unique reserves mined for Earthgreen’ s product line are located on Bureau of Land Management, U. S. Department of Interior owned land. The area under contract with the BLM will provide in excess of 10,000,000 tons of humate material from which all of Earthgreen’s products are derived.

An overhead view of our mining location in northwest New Mexico. The area being mined represents only a small fraction of the acreage under contract.

We mine only a small area at a time, and then we reclaim that area after the humate material has been removed, thus maintaining a harmony with the surrounding environment.

Our Production Facility

Earthgreen Products Inc.’s production facility is located in Northwest New Mexico, USA. It was founded in 1991 and continues to operate on a full-time basis under the management and control of principals at Earthgreen Products Inc. This allows Earthgreen to operate with complete vertical integration. In this way Earthgreen can provide a premium consistent product line to its customers both domestically and internationally.

Our state of the art screening and drying equipment (above) help to produce industry-leading products that deliver consistent particle size and performance, year after year.

Shipping

We ship to a variety of partners, both domestically and abroad. We’re pleased to be renowned for our consistently on-time deliveries to customers all around the globe. By managing the supply chain from mining to shipping, Earthgreen Products Inc. controls the consistent quality of its products, the timely production to meet customers requirements, and the premium economic benefits to our distributors and customers. We take great pride in delivering a premium product line on time to meet our customers’ requirements.
